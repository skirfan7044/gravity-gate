Passes & Ticket

Entry Pass

Price - ₹500

Entry fee includes five complimentary rides – Ferris Wheel, Bumper Cars, Carousel, Mini Train, and Splash Boat. Enjoy these exciting attractions without extra cost for a fun-filled amusement park experience!

Water Park Pass

Price - ₹599

Unlimited entry to wave pool, water slides, and lazy river. Includes locker and changing facilities. Best for visitors wanting a refreshing, splash-filled adventure.

Silver Pass

Price - ₹699

Includes entry with 5 complimentary rides: Ferris Wheel, Carousel, Bumper Cars, Mini Train, and Splash Boat. Ideal for a short, fun visit for kids and families.

Gold Pass

Price - ₹999

Access to all standard rides, water rides, and 7D Cinema. Includes one snack coupon and fun photo zone entry for memorable clicks.

Family Pack

Price - ₹3,499

Entry for four people with Gold Pass benefits, free family photo, and reserved seating at shows. Designed for families to enjoy a full day of fun together.

Adventure Pass

Price - ₹1,299

Unlimited access to thrill rides like Roller Coaster, Drop Tower, and Sky Swing. Includes 7D Cinema and adventure zone—ideal for adrenaline seekers.

Party Venue Plan

Price - ₹24,999

Book exclusive space for birthdays or celebrations. Includes decoration, music setup, 10 ride passes, and catering options. Perfect for hosting memorable parties inside the park.

School Pass

Price - ₹20,499

Exclusive school offer for 100 students! Enjoy thrilling rides, fun learning activities, and special discounts—perfect for educational trips filled with excitement, teamwork, and lasting memories!
